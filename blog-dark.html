Get Set for Your Event

How Eventbrite Works

January 20, 2019

Eventbrite is a website event management service. In minutes, you can organize your event, sell tickets, and manage registration for all kinds of events. Eventbrite can also be used for fundraising, house parties, any sort of musical performance or a concert. It works like an evite – it’s an online invitation. The difference between Eventbrite and other invitation systems is that Eventbrite is very professional, easily shareable, and provides results analytics. Eventbrite also allows you to charge for tickets. To get started with Eventbrite, we need to open an account. Creating an event is easy – we just input the date, time, place, and other information into Eventbrite’s system. Then we pick or design your own custom event invitation. Invitations can be customized to include our company logo and colors. Once the invitation is complete, we can email it to an email list or share it on social media. It is also important to promote the event on our website, blog, Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram in order to get the word out as much as possible!

How EventBrite Use their Keywords or Tagging for Ranking: Keywords are critical for the success of a paid search campaign. It’s important to use specific words (called “keywords”) on the website and ticketing page to help get found on search. Good knowledge and use of SEO is needed for an event to rank better when been search. According to a report I came upon in my findings, organizers of events do complain that there events are not easy to find this is due to the fact that they don’t include necessary keywords in their Eventbrite landing pages. The report provided solution that google trend should be utilized to research keywords and get relevant queries they use with the keywords in other to know how to better stuff the keywords in the landing page.

The report further state that, keywords should be placed in the title tag; which is the text that appears in the browser tab as well as listing when people google the event. Also, in the H1; which is the title that appears on the page itself, event name and possibly another keyword should be used here again while in H2; another variation of keyword should be added. Furthermore, the address of the event should be included in the page, but embedding a Google map of the event location will help Google understand that it’s local.

Internal promotional Tool: Recommendations at Eventbrite is an important internal promotional tool and this is based on user interaction of event (event views/purchases). Recommendation at Eventbrite is when the Eventbrite platforms brings to your notice similar events to the one you viewed or tickets you purchased and this is done by collaborative filtering. Cold start problem is a problem with collaborative filtering especially for new events. Eventbrite is an important platform that event organizers in the 21st century
